Photography

When my dad bought our first DSLR in 2010, I was just 6 years old, however I fell in love with photography instantly.

Among the pandemic I sought a hobby and thought, why not take some pictures while hiking.

All of the backgrounds on this website are original shots of mine and I have a separate page with most of my edited shots in high quality.

After upgrading from my dad's Nikon D3100 body with the kit lens, I now use a Sony A7 II with mostly the Sony FE 50mm f/1.8 prime. Alternatively, I use the included zoom lens, Sony FE 28-70mm f/3.5-5.6.
